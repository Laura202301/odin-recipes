Mixed Berry Galette

"I’m dreaming of berry season! One of my favorite things to make with berries is a pie or galette. I feel like it’s a dessert that really showcases their natural juicy flavor. Galettes are usually open faced, but I decided to decorate the top of this mixed berry one with a little extra pastry dough. I think It’s a nice finishing touch."

INGREDIENTS

Crust

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, leveled
  • 12 tablespoons butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 11 tablespoons ice cold water

Filling

  • 6 ounces blueberries, washed
  • 6 ounces blackberries, washed
  • 6 ounces raspberries, washed
  • tablespoon flour
  • 1/ 3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. To make the dough, add the 2 cups of flour to a large bowl. Then, add the cold and thinly sliced butter and use a fork or a pastry cutter to combine it until crumbs form. Next, add 11 tbsp of the ice cold water and stir until a dough forms. Add an additional tablespoon if it seems to dry.
  2. Shape the dough into a thick disk, and wrap it with plastic wrap. Place it in the fridge to chill for 45 minutes.
  3. While the dough is chilling make your filling. Rinse the berries and place them in a medium bowl with the flour, sugar and lemon juice. Stir to combine, cover and set aside.
  4. Once your dough has chilled, roll it out on a floured surface until it’s about 1/4 in thick, creating a large area that you can trim down to about a 12-13 inch circle. Set the scraps aside to decorate the top, or store them in an air tight container to freeze and use again later.
  5. Place your circle of dough on a large sheet or pizza pan lined with parchment paper and preheat your oven to 400 degrees.
  6. Spread the berry filling onto the dough, leaving about 2-3 inches of empty space around the diameter. Fold this extra dough over the filling, and place your galette into the oven to bake for 30 minutes - or until the crust is golden brown. **I like to brush the crust with a little milk or egg wash before baking to help it get nice and golden.**
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool a bit before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
